# PlantDiary — Design Context

## Audience
Home and hobby gardeners in Poland / Central Europe (climate zone 6a). Bilingual UI (Polish default, English). Used on phone in the garden and on desktop at the kitchen table.

## Use cases
Track gardens and individual plants, log watering/fertilizing/care, browse a large plant library (~14k) and rich bilingual plant profiles (care guide, diseases, photos), follow a seasonal calendar.

## Brand personality / tone
Refined, calm, considered — "Jony Ive" minimalism applied to a botanical product. Premium and quiet, never busy or toy-like. Confident use of color (NOT white-on-white), generous restraint, meticulous detail. **Zero emoji** anywhere — iconography is a single consistent line-icon set.

## Design system decisions (committed)
- **Aesthetic**: botanical-modern, editorial calm. Layered warm surfaces, hairline borders over heavy shadows, generous fluid spacing, left-aligned, asymmetric where it earns emphasis.
- **Color (OKLCH, tinted neutrals)**: deep forest green as primary; a warm "paper" background tinted toward green (not grey #F8F9FA, not pure white); cards a step off the background with hairline borders; one warm clay/amber accent used sparingly; functional red/amber for status only. Neutrals tinted toward the brand hue. No pure #000/#fff, no gradients-for-impact, no AI cyan/purple.
- **Typography**: display = "Fraunces" (organic soft serif) for headings; body/UI = "Hanken Grotesk". No Inter/Roboto/system defaults. Fluid type scale via clamp().
- **Icons**: `lucide-react` line icons, consistently sized/weighted. Every former emoji becomes a Lucide icon or is removed.
- **Motion**: subtle, ease-out (quart/expo); transform/opacity only; respects reduced-motion.
